    What are the benefits of digitising your organisation’s trade monitoring processes?

    Increasing regulatory scrutiny puts Compliance Departments under pressure. To mitigate the risk of market abuse, avoid hefty fines and conflicts of interest, Compliance Managers must ensure that adequate policies and controls are in place. A digital case management system for employee trading does not only streamlines compliance workflows, but it also ensures that all employees operate under full compliance.

    TradeLog is an employee trade monitoring tool with a strong track record in the Nordics. The online portal is now available for all European businesses that operate in the field of capital markets.

    Digital pre-clearance process

    Based on the rules set by your Compliance Department, the portal monitors and automatically pre-clears employee personal trades. You can also customise trading restrictions for different employee groups.

     

     


     

    User-friendly employee experience

    Employees can easily apply for pre-clearance, find out information on prohibited trades and administer their profile, including updating holdings and details of close connections.

     

    Simplified application workflow

    Process applications in no time via the “Applications Pending” tab. Follow up on missing consents or request additional input from the employee and other parties in the company, if required, before clearing the trade.

    Notifications are sent to the Compliance Officer and the employee, allowing swift action to be taken. TradeLog manages all necessary documentation related to the case within the secure online platform.
